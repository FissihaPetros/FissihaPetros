### 👋 About Me

I turn complex, real-world problems into **clean, scalable software** — working across the full stack with **ASP.NET Core**, **ASP.NET MVC**, and **Angular** to design and build robust **APIs** and enterprise web systems.

My approach is grounded in **Clean Architecture** and **Domain-Driven Design**, backed by strong **problem-solving and teamwork**. I'm equally comfortable with relational data using **MySQL** and **SQL Server**, and have hands-on experience with **WordPress** and **Drupal** for content-driven platforms.

- 🏛️ **Focus:** Enterprise-grade backend & frontend systems built to last
- 🎯 **Approach:** SOLID principles, separation of concerns, scalable-by-design
- 🏆 **Award-winner:** Bruh–ICT 2023 & Aybar Creative Person Innovation Competitions
